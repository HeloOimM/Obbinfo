What Are Obbies?

In Roblox, obbies are parkour courses that uses the Roblox Physics.

Obbies come in many difficulties ranging from effortless to humanly

impossible. Obbies also have different types that can affect gameplay

and obby difficulty.

Obby Types

Mega Fun Obby

The most well known and the easiest obby type. This type mainly

involves checkpoints with easy obstacles per stage. Usually, these

obbies are very long and can have thousands of repetitive stages.

Difficulty Chart Obby

This obby gets harder the more stages you progress. There are still

some checkpoints that can help you save your progress. Fun fact,

this obby type was inspired from ToDC in EToH.

Towers

Towers are structures that have obbies filled in. These Towers usually

don't have checkpoints, which means that you lose progress if you

fall. Towers can be CO(Adds special blocks that aren't in the studio),

Purist(Opposite of CO), and mixed gameplay. Towers also have

14 difficulties ranging from easy to nil.

Tiered Obbies

This obby only has 1 stage, but it is really hard. Tiered Obbies are

also purist and each difficulty is 2x harder than the previous

difficulty. The difficulties range from tier 1 to tier 25, however

the current human limit is tier 18.
